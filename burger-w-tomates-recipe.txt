Hamburgers with Cubed Tomatoes	
1 Tbl olive oil
1 pound hamburger shaped into 4 burgers
3 green onions sliced into 1/2 inch slices
1 small can diced tomatoes with juice
1/2 white onion diced
1/2 cup good red wine
1/2 tsp turmeric
1/2 tsp thyme
1/2 tsp cumin
1/2 garlic powder

Directions:
1) Saute white onion in olive oil until softened.
2) Add green onion and cook until softened.
3) Push onion to the side and add burgers.
4) Fry burgers on both sides remove fat if you like.
5) Add wine with spices
6) Add diced tomatoes and stir everything together
7) Cover and simmer until it reaches desired doneness say 20 minutes
